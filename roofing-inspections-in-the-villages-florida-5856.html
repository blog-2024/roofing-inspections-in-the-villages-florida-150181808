DIY Roofing Inspection Tips

Carrying out a homeowner roof evaluation may help homeowners spot potential issues ahead of they become serious repairs. Start by examining the roof at the ground level with a pair of binoculars. Scan for damaged shingles, signs of wear, or debris that may hinder proper drainage.

If you are comfortable to climb on the roof, ensure that you utilize proper safety steps. Use a secure gear and grip-enhancing shoes for improved stability. Check the flashing details around chimneys and skylights for any spaces that could result to leaks. Record your findings and evaluate reaching out to a professional if you discover major issues.
